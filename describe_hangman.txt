WELCOME TO THE HANGMAN GAME

=> You are playing against the computer;
=> You are trying to guess a random word chosen by the computer;
=> the word has between 5 to 12 letters and you have 8 incorrect guesses;
=> You are expected to guess only one letter at a time;
=> You can decide to guess the whole word at any time;
=> You can save a game or exit it at any time by typing 'save' or 'exit'.
